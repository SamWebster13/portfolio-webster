Experiment 2 - Living Impressions

Description

This generator creates a scrollable mountain scape with the early sunrise over steep and jagged cliffs, shading them in the early dawn mist. This project was meant to introduce us to the art capabilities of the p5 js library file, and how we can use it to make generative images.

Technical

The scene is built using layers of noise, each part of the screen representing a strip of jagged rectangles, similar to torn paper. The closer the mountains are, the more jagged and darker they appear, creating a sense of depth and distance. They're all drawn in a specific order, as layering is vitaly important in making the image.
